### Start by Defining What “High Quality” Means

To offer high quality services, you first need clarity. Define performance indicators such as:

* Incident response time (e.g. average response within 5 minutes)
* Patrol completion rate (percentage of scheduled patrols done fully)
* Client satisfaction / feedback scores
* Guard retention / turnover
* Training refresh frequency

This makes “quality” measurable and helps you track improvements over time.

Many security companies struggle with turnover; in fact, industry reports suggest turnover rates can reach around 30 % annually in U.S. security guard firms. ## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. What makes a security guard service “high quality”?

High-quality services focus on trained, reliable guards supported by proper supervision, technology, and regular audits. They combine people, processes, and technology to ensure safety and accountability.

### 2. How can I measure the quality of my security guards?

You can track metrics such as incident response time, patrol completion rates, client satisfaction surveys, and audit results. These KPIs provide measurable proof of performance.

### 3. Why is guard training so important?

Continuous training ensures guards remain skilled, alert, and compliant with site-specific procedures. It reduces mistakes, improves morale, and helps meet client expectations.
