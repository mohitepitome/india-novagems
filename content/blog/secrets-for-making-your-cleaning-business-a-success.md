## 1. Recruitment

Effective recruitment is the first thing every maid business should take into consideration. Remember, the type of talent you recruit directly impacts the quality of your services. As a result, recruiting the right talent to ensure unmatched service quality becomes very important.  

You can start with a thorough screening process to determine the strengths and weaknesses of your employees. Then accordingly, you can assign them jobs that best suit their skill sets and cleaning business KPI. Such an approach makes it much easier to get the [right recruits](https://novagems.com/data-driven-decision-making-in-cleaning-management/) and manage them effectively to ensure no wasted talent.

## 2. Training

Of course, not everyone is born with the right set of skills. Besides, finding the right talents and recruiting them is not always easy. So the best way to tackle this issue is to train your employees to enhance the quality of their services.

Training your employees is the best way to empower them and enhance service quality.
