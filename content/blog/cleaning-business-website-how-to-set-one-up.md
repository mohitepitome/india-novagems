[Cleaning businesses](https://novagems.com/the-ultimate-guide-for-cleaning-companies/) are no exception since a functional cleaning business website can help amplify sales and returns. But how to create one? It's simple! Just follow the steps to create a cleaning service website, and your cleaning business website will be up and running in no time.  Trust us cleaning business website setup is much easier than it seems.



### Step 1: Choose & Register a Domain Name

Of course, the very first thing you need to do is choose and register a domain name. Your domain name must represent your business and boost your website's SEO performance. So make sure you pick and buy the right domain name and register it before proceeding to the following step. And you are already half way through setting up a cleaning company website.

Select a domain name that reflects your cleaning business and is easy to remember. Opt for a domain extension such as .com or .net for better search engine recognition. Ideally, include relevant keywords related to your cleaning services within the domain name.



### Step 2: Choose a CMS (Content Management System) Platform 

Now that you have your domain name, it is time to start building a cleaning business website for your cleaning business. You can leverage the offerings of CMS platforms like WordPress or Wix to build your website for free. Besides that, you must make some efforts to evaluate the offerings of different platforms before settling for any one of them.    

### Step 3: Choose a Design / Theme For Your Website

Website builders such as WordPress and Wix are already equipped with existing template designs and themes. So the easy way here would be to choose from their available collection and get the ideal website in no time. But other than that, you can choose a customizable theme or design for your website and add specific elements that highlight your business in the best light.  

Plan your website structure by identifying the main pages you want to include. Common pages for a cleaning business website include Home, Services, About Us, Testimonials, Contact, and Blog. Create a logical navigation menu to help visitors navigate through your site effortlessly.



### Step 4: Define Your Website Structure

Your cleaning business website now has a unique look and feel that sets it apart from competitors. But a website with an appealing theme will do no good unless there you have some content to showcase. This is when you start adding web content and some appealing images to the website. 

Craft an About Us page that tells the story of your cleaning business. Share your mission, values, and team members' backgrounds to establish a personal connection with potential customers. Highlight any certifications, awards, or industry affiliations to build credibility and trust.

Feel free to experiment with the shapes and sizes of the images, along with the fonts for the content. You can even use some advanced [cleaning business software](https://novagems.com/how-to-choose-the-right-cleaning-service-software/) to generate appealing images and relevant content.     



### Step 5: Search Engine Optimization (SEO) 

It is great that now you have a functional and aesthetically appealing cleaning business website. However, your job here is not yet done because you are still missing one of the [most important ingredients of a successful website](https://novagems.com/3-pillars-of-a-successful-cleaning-business/). This is where search engine optimization (SEO) comes into the picture. And having a SEO-friendly cleaning website is a must in today’s digital era. 

Optimize your website for search engines by incorporating relevant keywords throughout your content, including meta tags, headings, and image alt text. Ensure your website loads quickly, is mobile-friendly, and has a secure SSL certificate. Regularly update your website with fresh content, such as blog posts, to improve its search engine ranking.

Simply having a [cleaning business](https://novagems.com/cleaning-company/) website is no good unless people can find your website on the internet. SEO will help make your website more discoverable and help your business reach the right customers to boost conversions. So make sure you focus on all essential SEO elements such as keywords, content, and more.     

 

### Step 6: Add FAQs and CTAs,

Next, you must add a dedicated section for frequently asked questions (FAQs). This will help resolve any common queries your visitors might have, which further helps enhance user experience. The better the experience visitors have on your website, the better the chances of them converting into [paying customers.](https://novagems.com/how-to-retain-customers-in-the-cleaning-industry/) 

Create a dedicated Contact page with your business's contact information, including phone number, email address, and physical address if applicable. Embed a contact form for visitors to submit inquiries or service requests directly from your website. Include a map or directions for easy navigation.

However, this will not be possible without a call to action (CTA) tab where customers can connect with your cleaning business with a click of a button. The CTA section must ask for important details about the visitors, such as their names, addresses, and contact details. This will give you a valuable lead that you can convert later.      



### Step 7: Ensure Your Website Is Mobile-Friendly 

The final thing you need to do before making your cleaning business website operational is to ensure it is mobile-friendly. This has become very important these days, especially in cases of cleaning businesses. 

Most people will want to access your offerings through a mobile device such as a smartphone or tablet. Hence, if they cannot access the website from the device of their choice, they will likely choose your competitors. So always make sure your cleaning business website is compatible with mobile devices.      



### Step 8: Promote Your Website

Now that your cleaning business website is all optimized and enhanced, it is time to launch it. Since your website is new, you must [promote the website across](https://novagems.com/how-cleaning-companies-are-using-technology-to-upscale-their-business/) multiple channels. This includes platforms like Google Business as well as other social media channels. 

Once your website is live, promote it across various channels. Utilize social media platforms, local directories, and online advertising to drive traffic to your site. Encourage satisfied customers to share your website with their network, further increasing its visibility.

This will help you market your cleaning business website to the right audience and enhance your reach to generate better returns. Also, update your cleaning business website regularly to keep people engaged and optimize the SEO performance. Optimizing a cleaning business website on regular basis is just as important as setting up a professional looking cleaning website.
