## Geofencing: Boundary Breakthroughs

[Geofencing technologies](https://novagems.com/gps-geofence/) redefine the boundaries of mobile patrols. By establishing virtual perimeters around specific areas, geofencing enables automatic triggers and alerts when a patrol officer enters or exits a designated zone. This innovation ensures that security protocols are consistently enforced, providing a proactive and adaptive security approach. I mean nobody wants their employees slacking off right? That will suck, big time. 

Now imagine if a security guard is caught not doing his duties properly. This will cause many issues. Like any criminal might use this to their advantage, the client may lose their trust. And what happens then? The business will lose their contract. Geofencing is one way to avoid that. It keeps the security guard where they are supposed to be and perform their duties.
