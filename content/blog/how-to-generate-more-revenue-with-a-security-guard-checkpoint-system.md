Guard tour systems can be divided into two main categories: **Wand Guard Tour Systems and Cloud Guard Tour Systems**. Both offer unique benefits and choosing the right system can directly impact your bottom line.

### Wand Guard Tour Systems

Wand guard tour systems are handheld devices that require security guards to physically touch or scan checkpoints during their patrols. These devices track guard activity and log the time and location of each checkpoint visit. While reliable and easy to use, these systems often have limitations, such as the need to manually download data from the device.

**Benefits:**

1. Easy to operate.
2. Suitable for small to medium-sized properties.
3. Provides a physical record of patrols.

However, wand systems are more traditional and may require additional steps for data reporting and tracking.

### Cloud Guard Tour Systems

Cloud-based guard tour systems use modern mobile technology, allowing guards to track their patrols via smartphones or other mobile devices. These systems store data in the cloud, offering real-time updates that can be accessed remotely by management or clients.

**Benefits:**

1. Real-time monitoring and reporting.
2. Data is stored in the cloud, accessible from anywhere.
3. Instant notifications of missed checkpoints or incidents.

Cloud-based systems are ideal for businesses looking for detailed reports, easy scalability, and added features such as GPS tracking and notifications. They also offer more flexibility and reduce the need for manual data collection.

### Beacons in Guard Tour Systems

Beacons are small devices placed at various checkpoints that emit signals, allowing mobile devices to automatically detect when a guard reaches the checkpoint. These beacons, often used in cloud-based systems, ensure accurate patrol tracking without the need for guards to scan or touch anything.

**Advantages of Beacons:**

1. Reduces human error in reporting.
2. Provides hands-free checkpoint verification.
3. Increases the accuracy of patrol data.

Beacons are particularly useful in high-security areas where manual interaction is limited. They enhance the efficiency and accuracy of the patrol system, leading to better security and increased client satisfaction.
