If you’re in the security industry in Western Canada, you're likely already marking June 11, 2025, on your calendar. That’s when [Security Canada West 2025](https://securitycanada.com/) takes place at the Sheraton Vancouver Airport Hotel in Richmond, BC, a key gathering spot for security professionals, manufacturers, and technology providers across British Columbia.

And this year, [Novagems](https://novagems.com/) will be attending.

We’re not going all-out with banners and booths. The goal is simple: real conversations. We’re excited to meet the folks who handle the day-to-day challenges of managing teams in the field. From last-minute shift changes, patrol routes, attendance tracking, compliance headaches—you name it. It’s where tools like [security workforce management software](https://novagems.com/workforce-management-for-security-company/) come into real focus, especially when you're dealing with the unexpected.

Security Canada West 2025 is one of those events where things feel less rushed and more personal. It's a one-day security industry event in Richmond, BC, but it packs a solid punch. There’s just the right amount of time to explore new tech, exchange ideas, and chat.

If you’re planning to attend, we’ll be there, so please catch up with us! Whether you’re managing a lean team or coordinating multiple sites, we’d love to hear your perspective and connect. We’re always learning from the people on the ground.

Catch you there!
