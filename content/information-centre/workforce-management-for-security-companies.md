### Core Components of Effective Workforce Management

#### 1. Shift Scheduling & Rostering

Create automated shift plans based on guard availability, site requirements, and compliance regulations.

* Include backups for absences.
* Rotate unpopular shifts fairly.
* Avoid excessive overtime on the same employees.

#### 2. Attendance Tracking & Verification

* Use GPS-enabled or geofenced check-ins.
* Record start/end times automatically.
* Set alerts for late arrivals or missed check-ins.
* Generate timesheets directly from attendance logs.

#### 3. Real-Time Reallocation & Communication

* Adjust guard assignments dynamically during incidents.
* Reassign idle guards across nearby sites.
* Send push notifications for updates or emergencies.
* Use centralized dashboards for supervisors to visualize live deployment.

#### 4. Analytics & Key Metrics

Track measurable indicators such as:

* Guard utilization rates
* Overtime and idle time
* Shift adherence and punctuality
* Site-level coverage compliance
* Attrition and absenteeism

Analytics convert raw data into actionable decisions that reduce waste and strengthen accountability.

#### 5. Integration with Payroll & HR

* Automate wage calculations and overtime.
* Sync attendance logs to payroll systems.
* Manage leave, replacement, and training all from one platform.

#### 6. Compliance & Certification Tracking

* Maintain records of guard licenses, renewals, and training certifications.
* Set automated reminders for upcoming expirations to avoid compliance issues.

Guard utilization rate, attendance compliance, overtime %, idle time, shift adherence, and client satisfaction scores.
