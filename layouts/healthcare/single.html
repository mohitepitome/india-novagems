Healthcare Staff Scheduling

Fast & Easy Scheduling, Zero Hassle

Effortlessly Create, Edit, And Share Conflict-Free Schedules In Minutes.

  • Drag, Drop & Copy Scheduling
    Quickly assign and modify shifts with drag-and-drop and copy-and-paste options. With the multi-view option on dashboards, medical staff can quickly shift to weekly and monthly views for better visibility.
  • Easy Shift Adjustments & Open shifts
    Create and use custom shift templates for common medical staff schedules, saving time across different medical teams. Quickly reassign shifts with open shifts and avoid last minute no shows or scheduling conflicts
  • Optimized Overtime & Role-Based Matching
    Record shift hours for all healthcare employees to prevent burnout and excess overtime. Easily manage multiple medical departments and clients from one platform, assigning roles and permissions to control access to sensitive data.
  • Instant Shift Alerts & Reminders
    Get automatic alerts to avoid scheduling conflicts and send automated reminders to staff to confirm shifts and minimize the chance of missed shifts, ensuring that all team members are prepared and on time for their scheduled duties.
