Key Features of Novagems Security Checkpoint System

Real-Time GPS Tracking & Geofencing

Ensure Accountability with Real-Time Monitoring

RTrack the exact location of your security personnel during patrols using GPS tracking. Our geofencing technology ensures that guards are on-site and following the correct patrol routes, providing you with real-time updates and alerts.

Patrol Scheduling & Shift Management

Streamlined Patrol Scheduling & Assignment

Assign patrols to security guards using recurring shift templates. The system ensures there are no overlaps or scheduling conflicts, making the management of your security team more efficient.

Incident Reporting & Documentation

Instant Incident Reporting with Multimedia Support

Allow your security guards to report incidents in real-time using photos, videos, and voice recordings directly from the field. These reports are instantly shared with managers, improving incident response time and ensuring accurate documentation.

Mobile App for On-the-Go Access

Convenient Access for Security Teams & Managers

The Novagems mobile app allows security staff to access their patrol schedules, clock in and out, and report incidents directly from their smartphones. Managers can review performance, track attendance, and adjust schedules on the move.

Automated Reporting & Compliance Tracking

Detailed Reports with Automated Generation

Generate detailed reports on patrol performance, attendance, and incidents. These reports can be automatically scheduled and exported for payroll or compliance tracking, ensuring your security operations are always audit-ready.

Multi-Site & Role-Based Management

Efficient Management Across Multiple Locations

Manage security operations across multiple sites from a centralized dashboard. The system allows you to assign specific tasks to each location, set permissions for different roles, and track performance across your entire operation.
