Keep Employees On Track With Automated Task Reminders And Real-Time Completion Alerts. Employees Receive Push Notifications When Tasks Are Due, And Managers Are Notified Instantly When Tasks Are Completed Or Left Pending. This Ensures Better Accountability And Workflow Efficiency. Selfie Verification For Task Completion

Ensure Employees Are On-Site And Completing Assigned Tasks With Real-Time Photo Verification. With Selfie Verification For Task Completion, Employees Must Submit A Live Photo Upon Finishing A Task To Confirm Their Presence At The Job Site. This Feature Prevents Falsified Task Completion Reports, Improves Workforce Transparency, And Enhances Overall Site Security.
