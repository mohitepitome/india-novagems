Clock-In/Out

Allow Employees To Clock In And Out Directly From Their Mobile Devices Without Delays Or Manual Paperwork. With GPS-Enabled Mobile Clock-Ins, Managers Can Ensure Employees Are At The Right Location When Starting Their Shifts.

Monitor Attendance

Stay In Control Of Workforce Attendance With Secure, Real-Time Tracking. Prevent Unauthorized Check-Ins With GPS-Based Tracking And Automated Attendance Logs. Managers Can View Who Is On-Site, Track Late Arrivals, And Ensure Every Shift Is Properly Covered.

Employees Can Log Their Breaks Directly In The System, And Overtime Hours Are Automatically Calculated Based On Actual Worked Hours. This Prevents Payroll Disputes, Ensures Fair Compensation, And Keeps Managers Informed Of Overtime Trends Before Costs Escalate.
