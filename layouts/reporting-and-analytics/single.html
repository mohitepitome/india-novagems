Shift & Attendance Reports

Get a complete overview of employee attendance and their shifts with detailed reports. Track late arrivals, absences, overtime, and no-shows, helping managers improve scheduling accuracy and ensure proper workforce coverage. This will increase your company's accountability and productivity.

Payroll & Timesheet Reports

Eliminate payroll errors and streamline workforce payments with payroll reports. Get visibility into worked hours, overtime calculations, break tracking, and pay rate variations for different roles. These reports ensure payroll is based on accurate data, reducing unnecessary labor costs and improving budgeting.

Custom Site-Based Reports

Optimize workforce management at specific job sites with customized reports. View location-specific attendance trends and shift performance in real time. These reports help security companies track workforce distribution across multiple sites, ensuring that each location is properly staffed and operating efficiently. Automated Report Scheduling

Set up custom reports to be automatically generated and delivered to your inbox at scheduled intervals. This ensures that key decision-makers always have up-to-date insights without needing to pull data manually. You can send reports within your own company and to your client as and when required.

Live Dashboard Analytics

The data-driven interface provides instant insights into attendance trends, overtime usage, and operational gaps, helping managers make faster, more informed decisions. Novagems also allows you to personalize the dashboard to focus on the data that matters most to you. Keep the fields that are important to you on the top for easy access.

Incident & Compliance Reports

Maintain a detailed record of workplace incidents and security breaches, ensuring that all reports are accurate, time-stamped, and securely stored. These reports help managers and compliance officers track trends, document corrective actions, and stay prepared. With one centralized system, all of your data is securely stored in Novagems and you can use them when required.
