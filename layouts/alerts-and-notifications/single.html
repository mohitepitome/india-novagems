Stay Ahead with Smart Alerts & Notifications

Shift & Task Alerts

Keep your team on track with automated shift and task reminders. Employees receive real-time notifications before their shifts start and when tasks are due, ensuring they never miss an assignment. These alerts help reduce last-minute scheduling issues, improve task completion rates, and keep operations running smoothly.

Missed Shift & No-Show Alerts

Stay ahead of scheduling gaps with instant alerts for missed shifts and no-shows. If an employee fails to check in on time, managers are immediately notified, allowing them to reassign the shift or call in backup coverage before it affects operations. These alerts help prevent understaffing, ensure client satisfaction, and keep security posts fully covered. Take your staff scheduling game to the next level!

Geofence & Location-Based Alerts

Ensure employees are where they need to be with geofence-based clock-in restrictions and alerts. If an employee tries to clock in from an unauthorized location, managers receive an immediate notification. This feature is important for field staff and remote job sites where location accuracy is critical.

Task Completion & Deadline Alerts

Stay ahead of deadlines with instant notifications for pending and completed tasks. Employees receive timely reminders to keep assignments on track, while managers are alerted when tasks are completed or running behind schedule. This ensures smooth operations and prevents last-minute rushes.

License & Certification Expiry Alerts

Avoid compliance risks with automated alerts for expiring licenses and certifications. With Novagems, Employees and managers receive advance notifications, allowing enough time to renew essential documents and maintain work eligibility. This feature helps organizations stay compliant, avoid penalties, and ensure all staff meets the required qualifications.
