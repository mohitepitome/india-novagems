Key Features of Novagems Mobile Patrol App

Real-Time GPS Tracking & Geofencing

Ensure Accountability with Real-Time Monitoring

Track your security personnel in real-time, ensuring they are on-site and following the correct patrol routes. Geofencing technology allows guards to only check in at designated locations, reducing time theft and improving security coverage.

Incident Reporting & Documentation

Report Incidents Instantly from the Field

Allow security staff to quickly and accurately report incidents using photos and videos. All reports can be immediately shared with management for faster response and proper documentation.

Smart Scheduling & Shift Management

Efficient Security Guard Scheduling

Simplify patrol shift planning with recurring and rotating schedules. The patrol app automatically checks for conflicts, ensuring there are no double bookings or overtime. Guards can be assigned to shifts based on their roles, availability, and compliance needs, making staff scheduling seamless and hassle-free.

Instant Notifications & Alerts

Receive Real-Time Alerts for Missed Check-Ins

Stay informed about your mobile patrols with instant alerts when a guard misses a checkpoint, arrives late, or changes their assigned route. This enables you to take immediate action, ensuring uninterrupted security coverage.

Mobile Access for Flexible Patrol Management

Empowering Security Guards and Managers on the Go

The mobile patrol app allows your security team to check schedules, confirm attendance, and report incidents directly from their smartphones. Managers can monitor performance, approve changes, and manage reports from anywhere, at any time.

Automated Reporting & Compliance Tracking

Effortless Report Generation for Payroll

Generate detailed patrol reports, attendance logs, and incident documentation with ease. These reports can be automatically scheduled and exported in formats such as PDF or CSV, making payroll and compliance tracking streamlined and hassle-free.

Multi-Site Management

Oversee Multiple Locations from One Platform

Whether managing one site or many, the mobile patrol app enables centralized security oversight. Assign specific tasks to each site, track guard performance, and ensure security coverage across locations with real-time data and reporting.
