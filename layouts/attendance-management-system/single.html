Employee Monitoring System

With an advanced attendance management system keep track of your employees and prevent time theft. Novagems provides an employee attendance application with complete features that ensure employees are where they need to be.

Key Features

  • Check-in/out
    Keep an error-free record of the attendance of your employees and always make your staff feel safe from understaffing. Know when your employees are starting their shifts, how much time they spend on one task, and when logging out.
  • Break-in/out
    Manage and record the break times in an easy-to-use interface and prevent employee burnout. Track breaks accordingly and boost the overall efficiency of the employees.

Location Based Tracking

Enhance security and accountability with staff location tracking to keep an account that your staff is where they need to be. Get instant updates if they leave the premises.

Key Features

  • GPS Tracking
    Get real-time updates and tracks of the employees when they are in the field. Have a connected team with Novagems field employee tracking app.
  • Geofencing
    Create a virtual boundary around your employees' working premises and get instant updates if and when they cross it. Protect the employees and the company against any false claims.

Workforce Scheduling

Still stuck in manual scheduling that is taking hours of your time? Now you do not have to fear manual errors. Novagems scheduling software for service business is specially made to make scheduling easy for better management of the workforce.

Key Features

  • Shift Planning
    Avoid last-minute cancellations and be prepared for anything. Easily create the employee's schedule. Schule jobs, assign tasks and manage the workload with Novagem's best field service management software.
  • Shift Templates
    Create schedules on the go with mobile-friendly interfaces. Publish shifts anywhere and keep everyone on the same page with instant notifications and reminders. Never miss an update!

Task Management

Streamline your operations with easy-to-use tools. Assign, track, and manage tasks efficiently to keep your projects on track. Boost client satisfaction and create a name in the industry.
