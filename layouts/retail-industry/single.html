Retail staff scheduling software

Create and manage shift schedules with ease

  • Drag, Drop, and Copy Scheduling
    Use drag-and-drop functionality to quickly assign and modify shifts, with the flexibility to switch between Table View, Weekly View, or Custom Views for improved organization. Set up recurring shifts and use custom templates to standardize shift structures
  • Track Overtime and Staffing Conflicts
    Track overtime in real time and receive automatic conflict alerts to prevent understaffing or excessive labor costs. Get open shift notifications to keep your team informed and fill vacant shifts quickly.
  • Shift Reminders & Notifications
    Send automated shift reminders to employees, helping reduce no-shows and last-minute conflicts while ensuring smooth operations.
Staff time tracking software and attendance

Track your staff’s working hours, breaks, and overtime effortlessly

  • Clock-In/Clock-Out & Mobile Time Tracking
    Enable employees to punch in and out via mobile or desktop, ensuring precise payroll tracking. Staff can easily track attendance through the mobile app, making time tracking effortless.
  • Shift Breaks and Time Off
    Ensure compliance with labor laws and store policies regarding break times and working hours. Keep a track of paid or unpaid breaks with the software to make proper timesheet.
  • Payroll Ready Timesheets
    Streamline the payroll process with automated timesheet generation and easy export options for accounting. Easily have all of the data in one place for better visibility.

Real-Time Staff Monitoring & Performance Tracking

Keep your retail staff at the right location with retail staff software

  • Geofence & GPS Monitoring
    Ensure employees clock in only at approved retail locations using geofenced zones. A live map view with GPS tracking lets managers monitor employee presence in real time.
  • Instant Alerts for Shifts, Tasks, and Attendance
    Stay informed with real-time alerts for shift changes, new tasks, approaching deadlines, missed shift tasks, and no-shows. Scheduling conflicts are shown immediately so you can take quick action.
  • Reporting & Payroll Accuracy
    Track employee attendance, punctuality, and absences to optimize retail staff planning. Manage timesheets and payroll with accurate work hours and overtime. Create custom, store-specific reports with shift-based payment calculation with precision.

  • Data Management
