Key Features of Novagems Staff Scheduling Software

Smart Shift Scheduling & Conflict-Free Planning

Fast & reliable shift planning assistance

Build recurring or rotating schedules using templates, employee roles, and availability preferences. The system automatically flags double-bookings, overtime risks, and compliance violations, making your employee schedule management software workflow seamless and error-free

Open Shifts Management with Instant Alerts

Keep your employee schedule management software error-free

Novagems helps you fill open shifts effortlessly. The system automatically identifies qualified employees based on role, availability, and compliance rules, then notifies them instantly via mobile alerts

GPS Attendance with Geofencing & No-Show Alerts

Ensure accountability with built-in GPS-based clock-ins

Employees can only check in when they’re at the right site, reducing time theft. Missed check-ins or late arrivals automatically trigger alerts, giving you time to assign backup staff, a must-have in any reliable staff scheduling software.

Mobile App for On-the-Go Access

Ideal for mobile teams, field workers, and multi-site operations

The Novagems mobile app empowers your workforce. Employees can check schedules, receive shift notifications, confirm attendance, and communicate, all from their phones. Managers can view real-time attendance and approve changes on the move.

Reporting & Export for Payroll

Digital analysis of time & cost, engineered for excellence

Generate detailed shift reports, attendance logs, and labor summaries in PDF or CSV format. Easily sync with payroll systems or HR tools to streamline end-of-week and end-of-month processing. These reports add extra value to your labor scheduling software.
