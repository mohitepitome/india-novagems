Scheduling & Shift Management For Cleaning Teams

Simplify staff scheduling and manage shifts with ease

Easily Create, Assign, And Manage Shifts Without The Hassle

  • Plan and Create Cleaning Schedules
    Effortlessly assign cleaners to specific job sites using drag-and-drop scheduling tools. View shifts in different formats—daily, weekly, or monthly—to suit your needs. Save time by creating recurring shifts and reusable templates, making the scheduling process faster every week.
  • Reduce Scheduling Errors
    Color-coded shifts help you quickly spot who’s working where. Receive instant alerts for any shift overlaps or conflicts, ensuring your team is always where they need to be. Cleaners can confirm or decline shifts directly, reducing no-shows and eliminating miscommunication.
  • Custom Views & Shift Templates
    Access real-time updates, employee responses, and shift statuses all in one place. These customizable templates help you stay on top of scheduling without needing to check in constantly, ensuring your cleaning operations stay smooth and efficient.
Employee Time Tracking & GPS Geofencing

Track work hours, breaks, and locations to prevent time theft

  • Clock-In/Out with Geofencing
    Enable cleaners to clock in and out directly through their mobile devices — but only when they are at the job site, using geofencing technology. Our cleaning company software ensures accurate time tracking and prevents off-site check-ins.
  • Track Shift Breaks & Time Off
    Automatically log regular hours, breaks, and overtime. Get instant alerts whenever a cleaner checks in, takes a break, or works beyond scheduled hours.
  • GPS-Based Tracking for Task Management
    Monitor your cleaning staff’s location throughout their shift. GPS tracking helps you stay informed and ensures that tasks are being completed on time and in the right areas.
